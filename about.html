About me

Pedro Vidal is a freelance graphic designer with 4 years of experience, specializing in vector animation (motion design) and social media. He has worked in both B2B and B2C environments, gaining extensive knowledge of the needs and expectations of clients from different sectors. His passion lies in creating dynamic and engaging animations for promotional videos, presentations, and interactive websites.

In addition to his animation work, Pedro understands the importance of social media for promoting businesses and brands. He helps create a strong and impactful presence on social networks, from designing consistent visual content to managing campaigns. Available for one-time projects or long-term collaborations, Pedro offers creative and high-quality solutions tailored to the specific needs of each client. He is always open to new challenges and creative opportunities.
